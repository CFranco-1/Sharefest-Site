About us

ShareFest was founded in 2007 by Gary Cheney, a New Lenox resident. He realized the depth of the need in his community and the surrounding areas and knew that he had to do something, so he started ShareFest. What was once meant to just help a few people, has expanded to not only make a difference but change the lives of thousands of people in its 10 year history.

Each year, ShareFest continues to grow and has acquired not only more volunteers, but also more partner organizations and dedicated committee members. There are so many ways to volunteer with ShareFest and show your support for neighbors in need.
